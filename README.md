KaloriPro is a high-performance, minimalist nutrition and weight tracking dashboard designed for users who want precision without the clutter. Built with React and Recharts, it offers a seamless dark-mode experience for monitoring daily energy balance and long-term physical trends.

Key Features:
Smart Macro Calculator: Automatically calculates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) based on your profile (age, weight, height, and activity level).

Dynamic Weight Tracking: Visualize your progress with interactive area charts that update instantly as you log your weight.

Intuitive Meal Logging: Quickly add meals with calorie and macronutrient breakdowns (protein, carbs, fats).

Real-time Progress Visualization: High-contrast progress bars give you an instant overview of how much of your daily quota remains.

Adaptive Goals: Toggle between weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain, and watch the app recalibrate your targets automatically.
